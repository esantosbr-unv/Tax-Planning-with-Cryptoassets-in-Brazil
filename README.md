# 🇧🇷 Crypto & Tax Planning in Brazil

This repository provides a structured guide and analytical resources on cryptocurrency taxation and regulatory frameworks in Brazil, focusing on fiscal compliance and optimization for investors and companies.

---

## ⚖️ Key Legal Aspects

1. **Normative Instruction RFB No. 1.888/2019**  
   Mandatory reporting by crypto exchanges domiciled in Brazil and by individuals/legal entities residing in the country whenever transactions are carried out abroad or peer-to-peer (P2P) and exceed BRL 30,000 per month.

2. **Capital Gains and BRL 35,000 Exemption**  
   Disposal of crypto assets whose total monthly sales are equal to or below BRL 35,000 is exempt from Income Tax for individuals. Legitimate tax planning studies temporal distribution and portfolio allocation within legal limits.

3. **The Challenge of Business Purpose**  
   To prevent the Tax Authority from disregarding corporate planning or asset restructuring involving crypto assets, the transaction must have a real economic justification beyond mere tax savings (substance over form).
